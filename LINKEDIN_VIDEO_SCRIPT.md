## 🎯 RECORDING TIPS

1. **Screen record** using OBS Studio (free) or Windows Game Bar (Win+G)
2. **Record at 1080p** minimum for LinkedIn quality
3. **Add subtitles** — 85% of LinkedIn videos are watched on mute
4. Use **CapCut** or **Canva** to add text overlays and transitions
5. Keep it under **90 seconds** — LinkedIn algorithm favors short videos
6. **Thumbnail**: Use a screenshot of the Dashboard with bold text overlay: "I Built This 🚀"
7. Add a **face cam** in the corner if comfortable — boosts engagement 3x

## 📐 VIDEO FORMAT

- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait) — takes more feed space
- **Resolution**: 1080×1080 or 1080×1350
- **Max file size**: 5GB
- **Max duration**: 10 minutes (aim for 60–90 sec)

## 🕐 BEST POSTING TIMES (LinkedIn)

- Tuesday–Thursday, 8:00–10:00 AM IST
- Post and engage with comments in the first hour
